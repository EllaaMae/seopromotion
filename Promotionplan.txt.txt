Honeybear
* What product/service is being promoted?
Honeybear is promoting children's clothing, made from soft, breathable and natural fabrics, focusing on comfort, being safe for sensitive skin and ensuring cuteness.


* Who is the target audience?
Parents are caregivers of young children and babies (0-5). Caregivers looking to give their children comfortable but stylish clothing.


* Which platforms are most suitable?
* Tiktok
* Instagram
* Facebook
* Online stores
* Pinterest


* Include 3-5 proposed hashtags
* #Cozycubs, #Softwear, #Honeybearkids


* Write a 2-3 sentence promotional strategy
* Honeybear will showcase the clothing in multiple settings with short videos and photos, and will have seasonal drops to create excitement and something to look forward to. Promotions will mostly be shown on online store and instagram and facebook.
